A Thread : Why NGO's Works?

{% comment %} {% endcomment %}

NGO stands for Non-Governmental Organization. NGOs work to make a positive change in various areas such as human rights, poverty, education, the environment, and more. They do this by raising awareness, providing resources and support, and advocating for policy change. For example, an NGO working on poverty might provide food and shelter for those in need, while also advocating for systemic changes to address the root causes of poverty.

A Thread on Philanthropist

{% comment %} {% endcomment %}

A philanthropist is an individual or organization who donates time, resources, or money to support a cause or multiple causes. The word "philanthropy" comes from the Greek word "philanthropos," which means "lover of humanity." Philanthropists are motivated by a desire to give back to their communities and to make a positive impact on the world. They may support a variety of causes, such as education, the arts, health and medicine, the environment, or human rights. Some philanthropists may focus their giving on a specific issue or geographic region, while others take a more broadly-focused approach.
